Here I attach two notes I took on March 16th and 19th, respectively. This is the short selling mechanism.

  • The Markit short selling data cover about 80~90% of the institutions offering the service, so there may be a certain institution who is not included in this sample.
  • Through the Markit database one can observe both lending capability and exact lending volume offered by participating institutions.
  • Usually one stock can be viewed under short sale constraint when more than 10% of the out-standing shares are shorted.
  • So far the papers published in journals have empirically demonstrated the negative relation be-tween the volume of short selling and the stock return (e.g. Jones and Lamont, 2002).
